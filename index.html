Fundamentals of Visual Design

Module-I
Drawing

In the second week of our journey in NID, we were introduced to the Fundamentals of Visual Design. I was not so much into drawing. Growing up, I avoided drawing as much as I could. And that made it difficult for me to accept the fact that I have to draw now, no matter what.
Even though drawing was a difficult task for me, visualising 3D things in 2D was a walk in the park for me, as I am coming from a Physics background, and as we do it all the time in Physics. So I could understand perspective drawing and isometric drawing. But soon, i realised that understanding something and putting it down in paper are two different things....
I thought this would be a difficult 2 weeks for me, but as i progressed into the course, it was a fun journey for me.
